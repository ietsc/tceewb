NEWSLETTER 2023

A Journey of Innovation and Community Impact

The IUCEE EWB TCE Student Chapter (IETSC) orchestrated a series of impactful events, each contributing to the holistic development of its members. Kicking off the calendar on March 1, 2023, the "Ignition" event served as a commencement, enlightening participants about IETSC's past activities, igniting innovative ideas, generating community-related projects, and showcasing these ideas on international forums.

TRANSISTION

This was followed by the "Transition" on March 16, 2023, marking the introduction of the new office bearers for the academic year 2023-2024, elucidating IETSC's motives, working procedures, and emphasizing the importance of Sustainable Development Goals in EPICS project identification. Following the ignition, the "Insightathon" on March 22, 2023, provided a detailed exploration of project ideation and membership drive, encouraging participants to fill out IETSC forms.

Problematics

Moving forward, the "Problematics" event on March 30, 2023, delved into the formulation of problem statements by teams, guiding them through the analysis of chosen problems, concept mapping, and arriving at refined problem statements. Subsequently, the "Scrutinize Event" on April 24, 2023, provided a platform for individuals or teams to approach problem-solving from multiple perspectives, optimizing outcomes and encouraging a distinct understanding of problems.

CRACK N CHILL

In a virtual mode on July 17, 2023, the "Crack n Chill" event entertained participants with non-technical gaming rounds, fostering camaraderie and testing quick thinking, knowledge, and observation skills. Continuing the trend, "Communicon" on August 17, 2023, focused on improving communication skills, promoting teamwork, and building confidence through engaging activitiest

Project Review

As the academic year progressed, the "General Meeting - Project Review" on August 28, 2023, facilitated detailed discussions on upcoming events, re-emphasizing the objectives and benefits of the chapter, and conducting comprehensive project reviews on ongoing projects. Following this, "Creathrive" on August 31, 2023, offered an intellectual journey with diverse events, cultivating critical thinking, sharpening observation skills, stimulating memory and insight, and promoting diversity in learning

Top News - Freshers were Introduced to Chapter’s Activities

The orientation event for the IUCEE EWB TCE Student Chapter (IETSC) took place on September 8th at KM Audi, where freshers were introduced to the chapter's activities and achievements. The event was a platform to explain the mission, vision, and significance of the chapter in detail, along with its unique features compared to other chapters. The presentation covered the chapter's achievements, involvement in IUCEE, and the events organized

HITAM INNO FEISTA

INCOGNITO

September saw the "Incognito" event on September 15, 2023, emphasizing effective leadership qualities, introducing the innovative "Leadership Pizza" concept, showcasing real□life applications of leadership attributes, and fostering inclusive leadership for collaborative teamwork. Subsequently, the national event "HITAM Inno Fiesta" on September 22-23, 2023, at Hyderabad Institute of Technology and Management showcased various competitions and aimed to raise public awareness while providing education, training, and challenging opportunities.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

The IUCEE EWB TCE Student Chapter held "Project Management Online Meetings" on September 29, 2023, and October 6, 2023, tracking the progress of diverse projects and ensuring alignment with objectives. Simultaneously, on September 21, 2023, "Co- Lead Recruitment" took place in the Medical Electronics lab, recruiting key positions within the chapter.

ELEVATE X

October witnessed the innovative "Elevate X - Inception" event on October 5, 2023, fostering entrepreneurship, interdisciplinary teamwork, presentation skills, creativity, and practical experience of pitching ideas to potential investors. Following this, "ElevateX - Budget Brainstorming" on October 12, 2023, focused on financial acumen, effective budget creation,investment attraction, project completion, loan repayment skills, inter-team collaboration, and excellence recognition.

MERCHANDISING

The series culminated with "ElevateX - Merchandising" on October 23, 2023, an online event challenging teams to navigate the complex landscape of marketing, emphasizing creative promotion, customer attraction and retention, profit optimization, effective communication, interactive engagement, positive relationships, and acknowledging exceptional achievements. In summary, the IETSC's diverse events throughout the academic year 2023 showcased a commitment to comprehensive skill development, effective project management, and a strong sense of community.
