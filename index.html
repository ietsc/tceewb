Student chapter information

The Student chapter of "Engineers Without Borders"-India was established at our Thiagarajar college of Engineering by on July, 2020. The objective of the TCE-EWB Student Chapter of "Engineers without Borders" India is to implement sustainable development Projects for the benefits of People, while training its members to become Project Mentors and Leaders with the motto of "Leadership through Service". Basic objective is to create Global Humanitarian Engineers and Global Leaders with Social and Moral values. In the pursuit of the objectives, the chapter will operate in accordance with the Constitution and bye-laws of EWB-India.

Vision

To serve the society by taking part in industrial, scientific, innovation, academic activities with competent professional minds with human values and ethics

Mission

To provide sustainable developmental solutions to improve the quality of life for common people.
To Train the future Engineers to solve Real time problems and motivate to become Global Leaders.
To develop the critical thinking, effective communication, creativity and Leadership qualities of Students.

IASF 2023

IASF 2023 is a flagship event conducted every year by IUCEE where students all over India will participate in this forum. This annual student forum is a part of the International Conference on Transformation In Engineering Education (ICTIEE). The IETSC Chapter represented TCE in this forum and won various prizes and created a strong impact about IETSC in that forum.
Date: January 4-6, 2023
Venue: Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering, Mysore.
